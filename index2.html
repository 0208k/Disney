Snow White

Snow White was living the life of a maid because of the jealousy of her stepmother, Grimm Hilde. Then one day, while singing in the well, the prince passing by heard the song and sang a love song to Snow White, who was embarrassed by the appearance of a stranger, secretly hid and listened to the song and fell in love with the prince.

However, Grimm Hilde, who was watching the scene, disapproved of the appearance and ordered the hunter to kill him, and suddenly, the hunter is in a situation where he is about to kill himself. While running away, Snow White finds stability again with the help of birds, rabbits, squirrels, deer, and turtles in the forest, and leaves the forest and stops at a dwarf house.

Rapunzel

Born as an only daughter princess, Rapunzel was actually a baby who inherited the ability of a magical flower that the queen, who was sick just before birth, drank with her hair.

One day, however, a woman named Godel breaks into the palace for eternal life and kidnaps her, claiming to be Rapunzel's mother, locking her in the tower for 18 years, and brainwashing her, saying there are many hair-wanting villains outside. However, Rapunzel began to dream of going out and seeing the lights rising from afar every birthday from a young age.

Bell

It has the characteristics of a strange character that is rare among Disney princesses. She is a beautiful and kind girl who likes to read books and sometimes fantasizes about romance novels, but the villagers treat Bell as a freak with their father.

At first Bell was afraid of the monster, and refused to have dinner with the monster on the grounds that he was forced to part with his father, but over time Bell finds a kind and humane side behind the monster's frightening appearance. The Beast also saw Bell as a tool to break the curse, but gradually fell in love with him and changed his wild manner.
